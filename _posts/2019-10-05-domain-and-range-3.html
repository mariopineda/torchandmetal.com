The volume of fuel, $v$, in liters (L) in a car's gas tank is given by the formula $v=60.5-0.072d$, where $d$ kilometers is the distance driven by the car.

  1. Using your graphing calculator or Desmos graph the relation.{% include_relative hint-modal.html content="Do not forget to label the axes and including units." %}
  2. Determine how much gas is left after driving 165 km. Round to the nearest hundreth.
  3. If 15 L of gas remains in the tank, how far was the car driven? Round to the nearest tenth.
  4. State the domain and range of this relation in both set and interval notations. Round to the nearest tenth.
