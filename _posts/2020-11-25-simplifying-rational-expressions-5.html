Express the following rational expressions in simplest form. State the non-permissible values of the rational expression as restrictions on the variable.

  1. $$\fraction{x-1}{1-x}$$
  2. $$\fraction{5x+15}{2x+6}$$
  3. $$\fraction{8y^2-4y}{4y-2}$$
  4. $$\fraction{x^2-2x}{2-x}$$
  5. $$\fraction{15x-5x^2}{3x^2-x^4}$$
  6. $$\fraction{10x-5x^2}{3x^2-6x}$$
