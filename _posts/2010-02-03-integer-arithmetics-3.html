Evaluate without a calculator:

  1. $$8 \div (7-9) \times (4+(-4))$$
  2. $$(8 \times (-4)-(-9) + (-7)) \div 3$$
  3. $$10+6 \div ((-7)-(-5)) \times ((-10)+5)$$
{% capture solution %}

A.

\[ \begin{array}{cl} & 8 \div (7-9) \times (4+(-4)) \\ = & 8 \div (-2) \times 0 \\ = & \fraction{8}{-2} \times 0 = 0 \end{array} \]

B.

\[ \begin{array}{cl} & (8 \times (-4)-(-9) + (-7)) \div 3 \\ = & \fraction{8 \times (-4)+9 -7}{3} \\ = & \fraction{-32+-7}{3} \\ = & \fraction{-30}{3} = -10 \end{array} \]

C.

\[ \begin{array}{cl} & 10+6 \div ((-7)-(-5)) \times ((-10)+5) \\ = & 10+6 \div (-7+5) \times (-5) \\ = & 10+6 \div (-2) \times (-5) \\ = & 10+ \fraction{6}{-2} \times (-5) \\ = & 10 + \fraction{-30}{-2} \\ = & 10 + 15 = 25 \end{array} \] {% endcapture %} {% include_relative solution.html content=solution %}
