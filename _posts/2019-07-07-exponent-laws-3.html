Use exponent laws to simplify the following:

  1. $4x^{-3}$
  2. $\displaystyle\frac{15y^{-3}}{5y}$
  3. $(3x^3y)(5x^{-2}y^4)$
  4. $\displaystyle\frac{24p^{-8}}{16p^{-3}}$
  5. $\displaystyle\frac{b^{4x+y}}{b^{x-2y}}$
  6. $\displaystyle\frac{x^{5a+7b} \times x^{3a+b}}{x^a \times x^{2a-7b}}$
