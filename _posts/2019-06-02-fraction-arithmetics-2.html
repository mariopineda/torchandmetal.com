Evaluate withouth a calculator by, when possible, reducing the fractions to lowest terms before multiplying.

  1. $$\fraction{10}{12} \times \fraction{9}{18}$$
  2. $$\fraction{3}{20} \times \fraction{8}{12}$$
  3. $$\fraction{2}{4} \times \fraction{5}{10}$$
  4. $$\fraction{4}{18} \times \fraction{2}{12}$$
{% capture solution %}
  1. $$\fraction{10}{12} \times \fraction{9}{18} = \fraction{5}{6} \times \fraction{1}{2} = \fraction{5}{12}$$
  2. $$\fraction{3}{20} \times \fraction{8}{12} = \fraction{1}{5} \times \fraction{2}{4} = \fraction{1}{5} \times \fraction{1}{2} = \fraction{1}{10}$$
  3. $$\fraction{2}{4} \times \fraction{5}{10} = \fraction{1}{4} \times \fraction{5}{5} = \fraction{1}{4} \times \fraction{1}{1} = \fraction{1}{4}$$
  4. $$\fraction{4}{18} \times \fraction{2}{12} = \fraction{2}{9} \times \fraction{1}{6} = \fraction{1}{9} \times \fraction{1}{3} = \fraction{1}{27}$$
