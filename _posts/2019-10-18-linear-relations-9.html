A ball is thrown into the air from the balcony of an apartment and falls to the ground. The height $h$ meters of the ball relative to the ground $t$ seconds after being thrown is given by $h=-5t^2+10t+35$.

  1. What is the maximum height of the ball?
  2. What are the x- and y-intercepts? Round to the nearest tenth if necessary. Explain what thease mean.
  3. What is the height of the ball after 3 seconds?
  4. At what time does the ball reach a height of 38 m? Round to the nearest tenth.
  5. When is the ball above 15 m? Round to the nearest tenth?
  6. Explain whether this is a linear or non-linear scenario.
  7. What is the domain and range, in set notation, of the relation in this context?
