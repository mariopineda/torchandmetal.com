Evaluate without a calculator.

  1. $$\displaystyle\frac{3}{12} + \displaystyle\frac{5}{12}$$
  2. $$\displaystyle\frac{11}{6} + \displaystyle\frac{2}{6}$$
  3. $$\displaystyle\frac{5}{2} - \displaystyle\frac{7}{3}$$
  4. $$\displaystyle\frac{5}{17} - \displaystyle\frac{2}{7}$$
{% capture solution %}
  1. $$\displaystyle\frac{3}{12} + \displaystyle\frac{5}{12} = \fraction{8}{12} = \fraction{2}{3}$$
  2. $$\displaystyle\frac{11}{6} + \displaystyle\frac{2}{6} = \fraction{13}{6}$$
  3. $$\displaystyle\frac{5}{2} - \displaystyle\frac{7}{3} = \fraction{15}{6} + \fraction{14}{6} = \fraction{1}{6}$$
  4. $$\displaystyle\frac{5}{17} - \displaystyle\frac{2}{7} = \fraction{35}{119} - \fraction{34}{119} = \fraction{1}{119}$$
