Evalue the following limits, if possible.

  1. $$\lim_{x \to 4} \fraction{9-x^2}{x+5}$$
  2. $$\lim_{x \to 8} \fraction{\sqrt{1-x}}{x-8}$$
  3. $$\lim_{x \to 7} \fraction{x^2-49}{x^2+7x}$$
  4. $$\lim_{x \to -1} \fraction{x+1}{x^2-1}$$
  5. $$\lim_{x \to 5} \fraction{\fraction{1}{x}-\fraction{1}{5}}{x-5}$$
  6. $$\lim_{x \to 10} \fraction{\sqrt{x+6}+4}{x-10}$$
