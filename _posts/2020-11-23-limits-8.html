Evalue the following limits, if possible.

  1. $$\lim_{x \to -7} \fraction{x^2-7x}{x^2-49}$$
  2. $$\lim_{x \to 4} \fraction{x^2+2x-24}{x^3-5x^2+4x}$$
  3. $$\lim_{x \to -2} \fraction{40-10x^2}{5x^3+40}$$
  4. $$\lim_{x \to -3} \fraction{x^2+3x}{5-\sqrt{1-8x}}$$
  5. $$\lim_{x \to 8} \fraction{\fraction{6}{x}-\fraction{3}{4}}{x^3-64x}$$
  6. $$\lim_{x \to 1} \fraction{x-1}{\sqrt[3]{x}-1}$$
  7. If $\lim_{x \to a} \fraction{\fraction{1}{a}-\fraction{1}{x}}{x-a} = \fraction{9}{16}$, then determine the value(s) of $a$.
