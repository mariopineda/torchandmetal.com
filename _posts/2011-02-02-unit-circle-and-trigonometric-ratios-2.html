Use the $45^circ-45^\circ-90^\circ$ triangle to find the exact trigonometric ratios of the following angles.

  1. $$\sin(45^\circ) = \sin \left( \fraction{\pi}{4} \right)$$
  2. $$\cos(45^\circ) = \cos \left( \fraction{\pi}{4} \right)$$
  3. $$\tan(45^\circ) = \tan \left( \fraction{\pi}{4} \right)$$
