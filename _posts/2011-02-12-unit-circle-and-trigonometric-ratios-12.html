For an angle $\theta$ in standard position, $\cos \theta = -\fraction{3}{7}$ and $180^\circ < \theta < 270^\circ$.

  1. Find the approximate value of $\theta$, to the nearest tenth of a degree.
  2. Find the exact value of $\csc \theta$. Rationalize the denominator.
