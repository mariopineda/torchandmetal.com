Given the function $y=4 \cos \left( 2x + \fraction{\pi}{2} \right)$.

  1. Express the function in the form $y = a \cos \left( b(x-c)\right)+d$.
  2. Describe the transformations that could be applied to the graph $y= \cos x$ to produce the graph $y=4 \cos \left( 2x + \fraction{\pi}{2} \right)$.
  3. Compare the following characteristice between $y= \cos x$ and $y=4 \cos \left( 2x + \fraction{\pi}{2} \right)$: amplitude, vertical displacement, range, phase shift, period and domain.
