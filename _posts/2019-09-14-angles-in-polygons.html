  1. Determine the sum of the interior angles of a decagon.
  2. Determine the number of sides of a polygon whose interior angle sum equals $4140^\circ$.
  3. The sum of five of the interior angles of a hexagon equals $600^\circ$. What is the measure of the sixth angle?
{% capture solution %}

A.

$n=10$, sum of interior angles $=(10-2) \times 180 = 1440^\circ$

B.

$(n-2) \times 180 = 4140^\circ$. Solve for the number of sides, $n$. \[ \begin{array}{lcl} (n-2) \times 180 & = & 4140 \\ n-2 & = & \fraction{4140}{180} \\ n-2 & = & 23 \\ n & = & 25 \end{array} \] The is a 25-gon (aka icosikaipentagon or pentacosagon), i.e. is has 25 sides.

C.

The sum of the interior angles in a hexagon $= (6-2) \times 180 = 720^\circ$. The sixth angle measure = $720-600=120^\circ$ {% endcapture %} {% include_relative solution.html content=solution %}
