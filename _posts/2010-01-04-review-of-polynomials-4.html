  1. $$\fraction{4x+8}{2}$$
  2. $$\fraction{9x+12}{3}$$
  3. $$\fraction{6x^2+4x-8}{2}$$
  4. $$\fraction{10x^2-15x}{5x}$$
{% capture solution %}
  1. $$\fraction{4x+8}{2} = \fraction{4x}{2}+\fraction{8}{2} = 2x+4$$
  2. $$\fraction{9x+12}{3} = \fraction{9x}{3}+\fraction{12}{3} = 3x+4$$
  3. $$\fraction{6x^2+4x-8}{2} = \fraction{6x^2}{2}+\fraction{4x}{2}-\fraction{8}{2} = 3x^2+2x-4$$
  4. $$\fraction{10x^2-15x}{5x} = \fraction{10x^2}{5x}-\fraction{15x}{5x} = 2x-3$$
