  1. $$ x+5=14 $$
  2. $$ 3x=-18 $$
  3. $$ 2x+1=7 $$

  5. $$ \frac{\displaystyle -3}{\displaystyle 4} = \frac{\displaystyle 6}{\displaystyle 4x} $$
  6. $$ -7 = \frac{\displaystyle x}{\displaystyle 2} - 2 $$
  7. $$ -5x-3=-8 $$
  8. $$ \frac{\displaystyle n}{\displaystyle 4} = 0 $$
