Simplify the following polynomials.

  1. $$3x+7+5x+2$$
  2. $$2a^2+5ab-6b^2-6a^2+8b^2-10ab$$
  3. $$15x^2y-4x^2-37yx^2+11+22xy^2-19xy^2+5y^2x-18$$
  4. $$(5a^2+8ab-4b^2)+(2a^2-15ab+6b^2)$$
  5. $$(2x^3-4xy^2+5x^2y^2)+(-3x^3+2x^2y-6x^2y^2)$$
  6. $$(7a-14b)-(2a-8b)$$
  7. $$(4m^2-9m+7)-(6m^2+5m-1)$$
  8. $$(5x^2-3y^2)-(7x^2+y^2)+(3x^2-2y^2)$$
{% capture solution %} Simplify polynomials by combining like terms. Final order of terms does not matter.

A.

\[ \begin{array}{cl} & 3x+7+5x+2 \\ = & 8x+9 \end{array} \]

B.

\[ \begin{array}{cl} & 2a^2+5ab-6b^2-6a^2+8b^2-10ab \\ = & -4a^2-5ab+2b^2 \\ \end{array} \]

C.

\[ \begin{array}{cl} & 15x^2y-4x^2-37yx^2+11+22xy^2-19xy^2+5y^2x-18 \\ & 15x^2y-4x^2-37x^2y+11+22xy^2-19xy^2+5y^2x-18 \\ = & -22x^2y-4x^2-7+8xy^2 \end{array} \]

D.

\[ \begin{array}{cl} & (5a^2+8ab-4b^2)+(2a^2-15ab+6b^2) \\ = & 5a^2+8ab-4b^2+2a^2-15ab+6b^2 \\ = & 7a^2-7ab+2b^2 \end{array} \]

E.

\[ \begin{array}{cl} & (2x^3-4xy^2+5x^2y^2)+(-3x^3+2x^2y-6x^2y^2) \\ = & 2x^3-4xy^2+5x^2y^2-3x^3+2x^2y-6x^2y^2 \\ = & -x^3-4xy^2-x^2y^2+2x^2y \end{array} \]

F.

\[ \begin{array}{cl} & (7a-14b)-(2a-8b) \\ = & 7a-14b-2a+8b) \\ = & 5a-6b \end{array} \]

G.

\[ \begin{array}{cl} & (4m^2-9m+7)-(6m^2+5m-1) \\ = & 4m^2-9m+7-6m^2-5m+1 \\ = & -2m^2-14m+8 \end{array} \]

H.

\[ \begin{array}{cl} & (5x^2-3y^2)-(7x^2+y^2)+(3x^2-2y^2) \\ = & 5x^2-3y^2-7x^2-y^2+3x^2-2y^2 \\ = & x^2-6y^2 \end{array} \] {% endcapture %} {% include_relative solution.html content=solution %}
