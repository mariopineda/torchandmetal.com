Consider the following pattern of multiplications: $$123456789 \times 9 \times 1 = 1111111101$$ $$123456789 \times 9 \times 2 = 2222222202$$ $$123456789 \times 9 \times 3 = 3333333303$$

  1. Use the number pattern to make a conjecture.
  2. Show two more examples that support your conjecture.
  3. Provide a counterexample to show your conjecture is false.

{% capture solution %}

A.

When the number 123456789 is multiplied by 9 and then by a whole number $n$, the product is $nnnnnnnn0n$.

B.

For example: $123456789 \times 9 \times 5 = 5555555505$, $123456789 \times 9 \times 6 = 6666666606$

C.

For example, $123456789 \times 9 \times 10 = 11111111010$
