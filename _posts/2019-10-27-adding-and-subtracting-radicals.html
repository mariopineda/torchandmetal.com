Simplify the following radicals.

  1. $\sqrt{75} - \sqrt{3}$
  2. $2\sqrt{24} + \sqrt{6} - \sqrt{54}$
  3. $2\sqrt{90} - 4\sqrt{40} - \sqrt{1000}$
  4. $\sqrt{900} - 1 + \sqrt{1}$
  5. $\displaystyle\frac{1}{3}\sqrt{63} + \displaystyle\frac{2}{5}\sqrt{700}-\displaystyle\frac{3}{2}\sqrt{28}$

{% capture solution %}

A.

\[ \begin{align} & \sqrt{75} - \sqrt{3} \\ = & \sqrt{25 \times 3} - \sqrt{3} \\ = & 5\sqrt{3} - \sqrt{3} \\ = & (5-1)\sqrt{3} \\ = & 4\sqrt{3} \\ \end{align} \]

B.

\[ \begin{align} & 2\sqrt{24} + \sqrt{6} - \sqrt{54} \\ = & 2\sqrt{4 \times 6} + \sqrt{6} - \sqrt{9 \times 6} \\ = & 2 \times 2\sqrt{6} + \sqrt{6} - 3\sqrt{6} \\ = & 4\sqrt{6} + \sqrt{6} - 3\sqrt{6} \\ = & (4+1-3)\sqrt{6} \\ = & 2\sqrt{6} \\ \end{align} \]

C.

\[ \begin{align} & 2\sqrt{90} - 4\sqrt{40} - \sqrt{1000} \\ = & 2\sqrt{9 \times 10} - 4\sqrt{4 \times 10} - \sqrt{100 \times 10} \\ = & 2 \times 3\sqrt{10} - 4 \times 2\sqrt{10} - 10\sqrt{10} \\ = & 6\sqrt{10} - 8\sqrt{10} - 10\sqrt{10} \\ = & (6-8-10)\sqrt{10} \\ = & -12\sqrt{10} \end{align} \]

D.

\[ \begin{align} & \sqrt{900} - 1 + \sqrt{1} \\ = & 30 - 1 + 1 \\ = & 30 \end{align} \]

E.

\[ \begin{align} & \displaystyle\frac{1}{3}\sqrt{63} + \displaystyle\frac{2}{5}\sqrt{700}-\displaystyle\frac{3}{2}\sqrt{28} = & \displaystyle\frac{1}{3}\sqrt{9 \times 7} + \displaystyle\frac{2}{5}\sqrt{100 \times 7}-\displaystyle\frac{3}{2}\sqrt{4 \times 7} \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{1}{3} \times 3 \sqrt{7} + \displaystyle\frac{2}{5} \times 10 \sqrt{7}-\displaystyle\frac{3}{2} \times 2 \sqrt{7} \\ = & 1\sqrt{7} + 4\sqrt{7} - 3\sqrt{7} \\ = & (1+4-3)\sqrt{7} \\ = & 2\sqrt{7} \end{align} \] {% endcapture %} {% include_relative solution.html content=solution %}
