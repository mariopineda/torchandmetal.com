Evaluate without a calculator and express in lowest terms.

  1. $$(-12)(-2) \times [-4+(-3)(-1)]$$
  2. $$\fraction{1}{2} \left( \fraction{2}{3}-\fraction{5}{4} \right)$$
  3. $$15 \div [(-4)(-4)+(-11)]$$
  4. $$ 2+\fraction{5}{8} \div \fraction{1}{4}-\left( -\fraction{1}{3} \right)$$
{% capture solution %}
  1. $$(-12)(-2) \times [-4+(-3)(-1)] = 24 \times [-4+3] = 24 \times (-1) = -24$$
  2. $$\fraction{1}{2} \left( \fraction{2}{3}-\fraction{5}{4} \right) = \fraction{1}{2} \left( \fraction{8}{12}-\fraction{15}{12} \right) = \fraction{1}{2} \times \fraction{-7}{12} = -\fraction{7}{24}$$
  3. $$15 \div [(-4)(-4)+(-11)] = 15 \div [16-11] = 15 \div 5 = 3$$
  4. $$ 2+\fraction{5}{8} \div \fraction{1}{4}-\left( -\fraction{1}{3} \right)$$ $$ = 2+\fraction{5}{8} \div \fraction{1}{4}+\fraction{1}{3}$$ $$ = 2+\fraction{5}{8} \times \fraction{4}{1}+\fraction{1}{3}$$ $$ = 2+\fraction{5}{2} \times \fraction{1}{1}+\fraction{1}{3}$$ $$ = 2+\fraction{5}{2} + \fraction{1}{3}$$ $$ = \fraction{12}{6} + \fraction{15}{6} + \fraction{2}{6} = \fraction{29}{6}$$
