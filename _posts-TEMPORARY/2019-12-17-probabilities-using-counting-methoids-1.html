Four cards are dealt from a deck of 52 cards. Determine the probability that the cards contain exactly two aces and exactly one king.

{% if false %}
$P(\mbox{exactly 2 aces and exactly 1 king }) = \displaystyle\frac{_4 C_2 \times _4 C_1 \times _{44} C_1}{_{52} C_4} = \displaystyle\frac{1056}{270725} 0.0039$ (0.4%)
