  1. The lowest common multiple of 33 and 110 can be written as a product of prime factors in the form $a \times b \times c \times d$ where $a$, $b$, $c$, and $d$ are prime numbers with a < b < c < d. Determine the value of $c$.
  2. Determine the greatest common factor of 6699 and 8265.
  1. \[ \begin{align} 33 &= 3 \times 11 \\ 110 &= 2 \times 5 \times 11 \\ LCM &= 3 \times 11 \times 2 \times 5 = 2 \times 3 \times 5 \times 11 \\ c &= 5 \mbox{ (Third number from left)} \end{align} \]

  2. $$ \mbox{GCF} = 3 \times 29 = 87 $$
