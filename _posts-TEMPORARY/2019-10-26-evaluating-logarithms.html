Evaluate each logarithmic expression without using technology.

  1. $\log_{2} 16$
  2. $\log_{3} \left( \displaystyle\frac{1}{9} \right)$
  3. $\log_{3} 81$
  4. $\log 1000$
  5. $\ln 1$
  6. $\log_{\frac{1}{2}} 8$
  7. $\log_{x} x^{-2}$
  8. $\log_{2} \sqrt{\displaystyle\frac{1}{8}}$

{% if true %}

A.

\[ \begin{align} \log_{2} 16 & = x \\ 2^x & = 16 \\ 2^x & = 2^4 \\ x & = 4 \\ \log_{2} 16 & = 4 \\ \end{align} \]

B.

\[ \begin{align} \log_{3} \left( \displaystyle\frac{1}{9} \right) & = x \\ 3^x & = \displaystyle\frac{1}{9} \\ 3^x & = 3^{-2} \\ x & = -2 \\ \log_{3} \left( \displaystyle\frac{1}{9} \right) & = -2 \end{align} \]

C.

\[ \begin{align} \log_{3} 81 & =x \\ 3^x & = 81 \\ 3^x & = 3^4 \\ x & = 4 \\ \log_{3} 81 & = 4 \end{align} \]

D.

\[ \begin{align} \log 1000 & = x\\ 10^x & = 1000 \\ 10^x & = 10^3 \\ x & = 3 \\ \log 1000 & = 3 \end{align} \]

E.

\[ \begin{align} \ln 1 & = x \\ e^x & = 1 \\ x & = 0 \\ \ln 1 & = 0 \end{align} \]

F.

\[ \begin{align} \log_{\frac{1}{2}} 8 & = x \\ \left( \frac{1}{2} \right)^x & = 8 \\ \frac{1}{2^x} & = 8 \\ 2^{-x} & = 8 \\ 2^{-x} & = 2^3 \\ -x & = 3 \\ x & = -3 \\ \log_{\frac{1}{2}} 8 & = -3 \\ \end{align} \]

G.

\[ \begin{align} \log_{x} x^{-2} & = y \\ x^y & = x^{-2} \\ y & = -2 \\ \log_{x} x^{-2} & = -2 \\ \end{align} \]

H.

\[ \begin{align} \log_{2} \sqrt{\displaystyle\frac{1}{8}} & = x \\ 2^x & = \sqrt{\displaystyle\frac{1}{8}} \\ 2^x & = \left( \displaystyle\frac{1}{8}\right)^{\frac{1}{2}} \\ 2^x & = \left( \displaystyle\frac{1}{2^3}\right)^{\frac{1}{2}} \\ 2^x & = \displaystyle\frac{1}{2^{\frac{3}{2}}} \\ 2^x & = 2^{-\frac{3}{2}} \\ x & = -\displaystyle\frac{3}{2} \\ \log_{2} \sqrt{\displaystyle\frac{1}{8}} & = -\displaystyle\frac{3}{2} \\ \end{align} \]
