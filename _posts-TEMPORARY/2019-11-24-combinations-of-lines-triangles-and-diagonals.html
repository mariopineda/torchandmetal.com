Given the following arrangements of points, with no 3 points collinear, determine:

  1. the number of lines that can be drawn through the points.
  2. the number of triangles that can be formed with the points as vertices.
  3. the number of diagonals that can be formed, assuming that a diagonal is a line segement connecting two vertices that are not adjacent to each other.
