Solve the following quadratic equations by factoring (when possible) and by using the quadratic formula. Verify the roots by graphing on the calculator. When applicable, answer to the nearest tenth.

  1. $x^2-3x-10=0$
  2. $x^2+7x-18=0$
  3. $-x^2-5x-10=0$
