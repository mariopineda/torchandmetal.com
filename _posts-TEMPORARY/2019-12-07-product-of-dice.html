Two dice are rolled and the numbers on each die are multiplied together. Round probabilities to the nearest hundreth and percents to the nearest whole percent.

  1. Create an outcome table for the sample space of products?
  2. What is the $P(\mbox{product is even number})$?
  3. What is the $P(\mbox{product is odd number})'$?
  4. What is $P(\mbox{product is} \leq 10)$?
  5. What is $P(\mbox{product is} > 10)$?
  6. What is $P(\mbox{product is an even number} < 20)'$?
  7. What is $P(\mbox{product is a prime number})$? {% include_relative modal.html content="A prime number is a natural number greater than 1 that cannot be formed by multiplying two smaller natural numbers." %}

B.

$P(\mbox{product is even number}) = \displaystyle\frac{27}{36} = \displaystyle\frac{3}{4} = 0.75$ (75%)

C.

$P(\mbox{product is odd number})' = P(\mbox{product is even number}) = \displaystyle\frac{27}{36} = \displaystyle\frac{3}{4} = 0.75$ (75%)

D.

$P(\mbox{product is} \leq 10) = \displaystyle\frac{19}{36} = 0.53$ (53%)

E.

$P(\mbox{product is} > 10) = P(\mbox{product is} \leq 10)' = 1-0.53 = 0.47$ (47%)

F.

$P(\mbox{product is an even number} < 20)' = \displaystyle\frac{36-20}{36} = \displaystyle\frac{16}{36} = \displaystyle\frac{4}{9} = 0.44$ (44%)

G.

$P(\mbox{product is a prime number}) = \displaystyle\frac{6}{36} = \displaystyle\frac{1}{6} = 0.17$ (17%)
