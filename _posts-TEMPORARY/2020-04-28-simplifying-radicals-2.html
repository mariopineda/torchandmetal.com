The simplest form of $\sqrt{8} - 2\sqrt{6} + \sqrt{50} + 3\sqrt{6}$ is

  1. $7\sqrt{8}$
  2. $7\sqrt{2}+\sqrt{6}$
  3. $\sqrt{12}+\sqrt{58}$
  4. $\sqrt{6}+\sqrt{8}+\sqrt{50}$
