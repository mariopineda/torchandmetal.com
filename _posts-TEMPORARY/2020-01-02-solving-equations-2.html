Solve and verify:

  1. $$\fraction{t}{1.6}+5.9=-3.2$$
  2. $$2.05=0.9x-6.5$$
  3. $$\fraction{2}{5} = \fraction{2}{3} - \fraction{r}{5}$$
  4. $$2\fraction{1}{2}+1\fraction{1}{6}v=-\fraction{5}{6}$$
  5. $$-3.3g+1.5=-8.4$$

{% capture solution %}

A.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} \fraction{t}{1.6}+5.9 & = & -3.2 \\ \fraction{t}{1.6} & = & -3.2-5.9 \\ \fraction{t}{1.6} & = & -9.1 \\ t & = & -9.1 \times 1.6 \\ t & = & -14.56 \end{array} \]

B.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 2.05 & = & 0.9x-6.5 \\ -0.9x & = & -6.5-2.05 \\ -0.9x & = & -8.55 \\ x & = & -8.55 \div (-0.9) \\ x & = & 9.5 \end{array} \]

C.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} \fraction{2}{5} & = & \fraction{2}{3} - \fraction{r}{5} \\ \fraction{2}{5} - \fraction{2}{3} & = & -\fraction{r}{5} \\ \fraction{6}{15} - \fraction{10}{15} & = & -\fraction{r}{5} \\ -\fraction{4}{15} & = & -\fraction{r}{5} \\ r & = & -5 \left( -\fraction{4}{15} \right) \\ r & = & \fraction{4}{3} \end{array} \]

D.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 2\fraction{1}{2}+1\fraction{1}{6}v & = & -\fraction{5}{6} \\ \fraction{5}{2}+\fraction{7}{6}v & = & -\fraction{5}{6} \\ \fraction{7}{6}v & = & -\fraction{5}{6}-\fraction{5}{2} \\ \fraction{7}{6}v & = & -\fraction{5}{6}-\fraction{15}{6} \\ \fraction{7}{6}v & = & -\fraction{20}{6} \\ v & = & \left( -\fraction{20}{6} \right) \times \fraction{6}{7} \\ v & = & -\fraction{20}{7} \end{array} \]

E.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} -3.3g+1.5 & = & -8.4 \\ -3.3g & = & -8.4 - 1.5 \\ -3.3g & = & -9.9 \\ g & = & -9.9 \div (-3.3) \\ g & = & 3 \end{array} \] {% endcapture %} {% include_relative solution.html content=solution %}
