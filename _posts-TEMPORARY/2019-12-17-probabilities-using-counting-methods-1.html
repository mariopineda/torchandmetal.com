Determine the following probabilities to the nearest tenth of a percent.

  1. The probability of randomly selecting only boys to be on a four person committee out of five boys and six girls.
  2. The probability of randomly selecting an equal number of boys and girls to be on a four person committee out of five boys and six girls.
  3. The probability of randomly selecting more girls than boys on a four person committee out of five boys and six girls.
  4. Four cards are dealt from a deck of 52 cards. Determine the probability that the cards contain exactly two aces and exactly one king.
{% if false %}

A.

$P(\mbox{exactly 2 aces and exactly 1 king }) = \displaystyle\frac{_4 C_2 \times _4 C_1 \times _{44} C_1}{_{52} C_4} = \displaystyle\frac{1056}{270725} 0.0039$ (0.4%)
