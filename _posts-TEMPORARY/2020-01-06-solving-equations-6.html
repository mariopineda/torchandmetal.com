Solve:

  1. $$-3+\fraction{y}{5} = -12$$
  2. $$1+\fraction{y}{-3} = 9$$
  3. $$\fraction{x}{3}+(-4) = -1$$
  4. $$-2+\fraction{z}{-5} = 3$$
  5. $$\fraction{a}{6}-(-8) = 12$$

{% capture solution %}

A.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} -3+\fraction{y}{5} & = & -12 \\ \fraction{y}{5} & = & -12+3 \\ \fraction{y}{5} & = & -9 \\ y & = & -9 \times 5 \\ y & = & -45 \end{array} \]

B.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 1+\fraction{y}{-3} & = & 9 \\ \fraction{3}{-3} & = & 9-1 \\ \fraction{3}{-3} & = & 8 \\ y & = & 8(-3) \\ y & = & -24 \end{array} \]

C.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} \fraction{x}{3}+(-4) & = & -1 \\ \fraction{x}{3}-4 & = & -1 \\ \fraction{x}{3} & = & -1+4 \\ \fraction{x}{3} & = & 3 \\ x & = & 3 \times 3 \\ x & = & 9 \end{array} \]

D.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} -2+\fraction{z}{-5} & = & 3 \\ \fraction{z}{-5} & = & 3+2 \\ \fraction{z}{-5} & = & 5 \\ z & = & 5(-5) \\ z & = & -25 \end{array} \]

E.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} \fraction{a}{6}-(-8) & = & 12 \\ \fraction{a}{6}+8 & = & 12 \\ \fraction{a}{6} & = & 12-8 \\ \fraction{a}{6} & = & 4 \\ a & = & a \times 6 \\ a & = & 24 \end{array} \] {% endcapture %} {% include_relative solution.html content=solution %}
