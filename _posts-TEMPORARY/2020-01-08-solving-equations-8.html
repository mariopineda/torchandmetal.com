Solve:

  1. $$6x-3 = 9x+2$$
  2. $$\fraction{3}{x} = \fraction{5}{4x}$$
  3. $$4x+2(5-x) = -7$$
  4. $$\fraction{1}{3}x-8 = \fraction{5}{3}(x+6)$$
  5. $$11x-\fraction{4}{5}(x+3) = -4$$

{% capture solution %}

A.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 6x-3 & = & 9x+2 \\ 6x-9x & = & 2+3 \\ -3x & = & 5 \\ x & = & -\fraction{5}{3} \end{array} \]

B.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} \fraction{3}{x} & = & \fraction{5}{4x} \\ \fraction{x}{3} & = & \fraction{4x}{5} \\ 5x & = & 3(4x) \\ 5x & = & 12x \\ 5x-12x & = & 0 \\ -7x & = & 0 \\ x & = & \fraction{0}{-7} \\ x & = & 0 \end{array} \]

C.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 4x+2(5-x) & = & -7 \\ 4x+1-2x & = & -7 \\ 2x & = & -8-10 \\ 2x & = & -17 \\ x & = & -\fraction{17}{2} \end{array} \]

D.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} \fraction{1}{3}x-8 & = & \fraction{5}{3}(x+6) \\ x-24 & = & \fraction{15}{3}(x+6) \\ 3 \times \left[ \fraction{1}{3}x-8 \right] & = & 3 \times \left[ \fraction{5}{3}(x+6) \right] \\ x-24 & = & 15x+90 \\ x-15x & = & 90+24 \\ -14x & = & 114 \\ x & = & -\fraction{114}{14} \\ x & = & -10 \end{array} \]

E.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 11x-\fraction{4}{5}(x+3) & = & -4 \\ 5 \times \left[ 11x-\fraction{4}{5}(x+3) \right] & = & 5 \times \left[ -4 \right] \\ 55x-4(x+3) & = & -20 \\ 55x-4x-12 & = & -20 \\ 51x & = & -20+12 \\ 51x & = & -8 \\ x & = & -\fraction{8}{51} \end{array} \] {% endcapture %} {% include_relative solution.html content=solution %}
