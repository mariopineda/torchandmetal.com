Algebraically solve the following exponential equations using logarithms. Round your answer to the nearest thousandth.

  1. $117=3 \times 2^x$
  2. $3=8^{2x-1}$
  3. $\left( \displaystyle\frac{1}{3} \right)^x - 40 = 0$
  4. $\left( \displaystyle\frac{2}{5} \right)^{-x} = 5$
  5. $3^{x+2} = 5(5^{x-1})$
