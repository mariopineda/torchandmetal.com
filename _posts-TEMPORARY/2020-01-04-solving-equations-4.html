Solve:

  1. $$\fraction{1}{5}n+\fraction{3}{2}=\fraction{3}{10}n$$
  2. $$-0.25f = 0.35-0.45f$$
  3. $$12.4g+34.2=9.5-3.1g$$
  4. $$1.4(2h+3) = 0.9(3h-2)$$
  5. $$\fraction{3v+2}{3} = \fraction{2v-1}{4}$$

{% capture solution %}

A.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} \fraction{1}{5}n+\fraction{3}{2} & = & \fraction{3}{10}n \\ \fraction{1}{5}n-\fraction{3}{10}n & = & -\fraction{3}{2} \\ \fraction{2}{10}n-\fraction{3}{10}n & = & -\fraction{3}{2} \\ -\fraction{1}{10}n & = & -\fraction{3}{2} \\ n & = & \left( -\fraction{3}{2} \right) \left( -\fraction{10}{1}\right) \\ n & = & \fraction{30}{2} \\ n & = & 15 \end{array} \]

B.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} -0.25f & = & 0.35-0.45f \\ -0.25f+0.45f & = & 0.35 \\ 0.2f & = & 0.35 \\ f & = & 0.35 \div 0.2 \\ f & = & 1.75 \end{array} \]

C.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 12.4g+34.2 & = & 9.5-3.1g \\ 12.4g+3.1g & = & 9.5-34.2 \\ 15.5g & = & -24.7 \\ g & = & -24.7 \div 15.5 \\ g & = & -1.59 \end{array} \]

D.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 1.4(2h+3) & = & 0.9(3h-2) \\ 2.8h+4.2 & = & 2.7h-1.8 \\ 2.8h-2.7h & = & -1.8-4.2 \\ 0.1h & = & -6 \\ h & = & -6 \div 0.1 \\ h & = & -60 \end{array} \]

E.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} \fraction{3v+2}{3} & = & \fraction{2v-1}{4} \\ \fraction{3v}{3}+\fraction{2}{3} & = & \fraction{2v}{4}-\fraction{1}{4} \\ \fraction{v}{1}+\fraction{2}{3} & = & \fraction{v}{2}-\fraction{1}{4} \\ \fraction{v}{1}-\fraction{v}{2} & = & -\fraction{1}{4}-\fraction{2}{3} \\ \fraction{2v}{2}-\fraction{v}{2} & = & -\fraction{3}{12}-\fraction{8}{12} \\ \fraction{v}{2} & = & -\fraction{11}{12} \\ v & = & -\fraction{11}{12} \times 2 \\ v & = & -\fraction{22}{12} = -\fraction{11}{6} \end{array} \] {% endcapture %} {% include_relative solution.html content=solution %}
