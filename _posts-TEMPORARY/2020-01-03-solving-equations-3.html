Solve:

  1. $$3(5.8+e)=-2.7$$
  2. $$-\fraction{5}{6} = \fraction{r-4}{3}$$
  3. $$10.1 = \fraction{0.9+h}{-2}$$
  4. $$-\fraction{4}{5}(q+1)=-1\fraction{1}{2}$$
  5. $$0.11=-3(1.93+m)$$

{% capture solution %}

A.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 3(5.8+e) & = & -2.7 \\ 17.4 + 3e & = & -2.7 \\ 3e & = & -2.7-17.4 \\ 3e & = & -20.1 \\ e & = & -20.1 \div 3 \\ e & = & -6.7 \end{array} \]

B.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} -\fraction{5}{6} & = & \fraction{r-4}{3} \\ 3 \left( -\fraction{5}{6} \right) & = & r-4 \\ -\fraction{15}{6} & = & r-4 \\ r & = & -\fraction{15}{6} + 4 \\ r & = & -\fraction{15}{6} + \fraction{24}{6} \\ r & = & \fraction{9}{6} \\ r & = & \fraction{3}{2} \end{array} \]

C.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 10.1 & = & \displaystyle\frac{0.9+h}{-2} \\ 10.1(-2) & = & 0.9+h \\ -20.2 & = & 0.9+h \\ -20.2-0.9 & = & h \\ -21.1 & = & h \end{array} \]

D.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} -\fraction{4}{5}(q+1) & = & -1\fraction{1}{2} \\ -\fraction{4}{5}q-\fraction{4}{5} & = & -\fraction{1}{2} \\ -\fraction{4}{5}q & = & -\fraction{1}{2}+\fraction{4}{5} \\ -\fraction{4}{5}q & = & -\fraction{5}{10}+\fraction{8}{10} \\ -\fraction{4}{5}q & = & \fraction{3}{10} \\ q & = & \fraction{3}{10} \left( -\fraction{5}{4} \right) \\ q & = & -\fraction{15}{40} \\ q & = & -\fraction{3}{8} \end{array} \]

E.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 0.11 & = & -3(1.93+m) \\ 0.11 & = & -5.79-3m \\ 0.11+5.76 & = & -3m \\ 5.9 & = & -3m \\ 5.9 \div (-3) & = & m & \\ -1.97 & = & m \end{array} \] {% endcapture %} {% include_relative solution.html content=solution %}
