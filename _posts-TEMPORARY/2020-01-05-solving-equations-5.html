Solve:

  1. $$2(3-h)-6 = -5h$$
  2. $$3(1-3g) = -7+g$$
  3. $$2z+6 = 3z+1$$
  4. $$6t-5 = -9t-9$$
  5. $$-6k+1 = -2+7k$$

{% capture solution %}

A.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 2(3-h)-6 & = & -5h \\ -2h & = & -5h \\ 0 & = & -5h+2h \\ 0 & = & -3h \\ \fraction{0}{-3} & = & h \\ 0 &= & h \end{array} \]

B.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 3(1-3g) & = & -7+g \\ 3-9g & = & -7+g \\ -9g-g & = & -7-3 \\ -10g & = & -10 \\ g & = & \fraction{-10}{-10} \\ g & = & 1 \end{array} \]

C.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 2z+6 & = & 3z+1 \\ 2z-3z & = & 1-6 \\ -1z & = & -5 \\ z & = & \fraction{-5}{-1} \\ z & = & 5 \end{array} \]

D.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 6t-5 & = & -9t-9 \\ 6t+9t & = & -9+5 \\ 15t & = & -4 \\ t & = & -\fraction{4}{15} \end{array} \]

E.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} -6k+1 & = & -2+7k \\ -6k-7k & = & -2-1 \\ -13k & = & -3 \\ k & = & \fraction{-3}{-13} \\ k & = & \fraction{3}{13} \end{array} \] {% endcapture %} {% include_relative solution.html content=solution %}
