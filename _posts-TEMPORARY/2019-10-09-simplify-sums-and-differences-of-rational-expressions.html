Simplify the following rational expressions.

  1. $$\displaystyle\frac{1}{a+1} + \displaystyle\frac{1}{a-1}$$
  2. $$\displaystyle\frac{p-1}{p+2} + \displaystyle\frac{p+2}{p+3}$$
  3. $$\displaystyle\frac{5m+1}{m(m-7)} + \displaystyle\frac{4m+8}{m(7-m)}$$
  4. $$\displaystyle\frac{5}{x+2} - \displaystyle\frac{2}{x+5}$$
  5. $$\displaystyle\frac{3y-1}{3y-2} - \displaystyle\frac{3y-2}{3y+1}$$
  6. $$\displaystyle\frac{3}{25-a^2} - \displaystyle\frac{a+5}{2a-10}$$
{% capture solution %}

A.

$$ \begin{array}{rll} & \displaystyle\frac{1}{a+1} + \displaystyle\frac{1}{a-1} & \mbox{LCD}=(a-1)(a+1) \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{1(a-1) + 1(a+1)}{(a+1)(a-1)} & \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{a-1+a+1}{(a+1)(a-1)} & \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{2a}{(a+1)(a-1)}, a \neq \pm 1 & \\ \end{array} $$

B.

$$ \begin{array}{rll} & \displaystyle\frac{p-1}{p+2} + \displaystyle\frac{p+2}{p+3} & \mbox{LCD}=(p+2)(p+3) \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{(p-1)(p+3)+(p+2)(p+2)}{(p+2)(p+3)} & \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{p^2+3p-p-3+p^2+2p+2p+4}{(p+2)(p+3)} & \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{2p^2+6p+1}{(p+2)(p+3)}, p \neq -3, -2 & \\ \end{array} $$

C.

$$ \begin{array}{rll} & \displaystyle\frac{5m+1}{m(m-7)} + \displaystyle\frac{4m+8}{m(7-m)} & \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{5m+1}{m(m-7)} + \displaystyle\frac{4m+8}{-m(m-7)} & \mbox{LCD}=-m(m-7)\\ = & \displaystyle\frac{-(5m+1)+(4m+8)}{-m(m-7)} & \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{-5m-1+4m+8}{-m(m-7)} & \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{-m+7}{-m(m-7)} & \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{-1}{-m} & \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{1}{m}, m \neq 0,7 & \\ \end{array} $$

D.

$$ \begin{array}{rll} & \displaystyle\frac{5}{x+2} - \displaystyle\frac{2}{x+5} & \mbox{LCD}=(x+2)(x+5) \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{5(x+5)-2(x+2)}{(x+2)(x+5)} & \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{5x+25-2x-4}{(x+2)(x+5)} & \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{3x+21}{(x+2)(x+5)} & \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{3(x+7)}{(x+2)(x+5)}, x \neq -5, -2 & \\ \end{array} $$

E.

$$ \begin{array}{rll} & \displaystyle\frac{3y-1}{3y-2} - \displaystyle\frac{3y-2}{3y+1} & \mbox{LCD}=(3y-2)(3y+1) \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{3y-1-(3y-2)}{(3y-2)(3y+1)} & \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{3y-1-3y+2}{(3y-2)(3y+1)} & \\ = & \displaystyle\frac{1}{(3y-2)(3y+1)}, y \neq \displaystyle\frac{2}{3}, -\displaystyle\frac{1}{3} & \\ \end{array} $$
