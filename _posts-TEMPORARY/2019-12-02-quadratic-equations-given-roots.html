Determine possible quadratic (or cubic) equations that have the following given roots. Express equations in expanded form.

  1. $\pm 2$
  2. $5$ and $-1$
  3. $\pm \displaystyle\frac{2}{3}$
  4. $-\displaystyle\frac{1}{2}$ and $\displaystyle\frac{7}{2}$
  5. $0$, $2$ and $8$ {% include_relative modal.html content="Cubic equations have three roots" %}
{% if true %}
Answers may vary.
  1. $\pm 2 \Rightarrow$ $(x+2)(x-2)=0 \Rightarrow$ $(x^2-4)=0$
  2. $5$ and $-1 \Rightarrow$ $(x-5)(x+1)=0 \Rightarrow$ $x^2-4x-5=0$
  3. $\pm \displaystyle\frac{2}{3} \Rightarrow$ $\left(x+\displaystyle\frac{2}{3}\right)\left(x-\displaystyle\frac{2}{3}\right)=0 \Rightarrow$ $\left(x^2 - \displaystyle\frac{4}{9}\right)=0$
  4. $-\displaystyle\frac{1}{2}$ and $\displaystyle\frac{7}{2} \Rightarrow$ $\left( x+\displaystyle\frac{1}{2} \right) \left( x-\displaystyle\frac{7}{2}\right)=0 \Rightarrow$ $x^2-3x-\displaystyle\frac{7}{4}=0$
  5. $0$, $2$ and $8 \Rightarrow$ $x(x-2)(x-8)=0 \Rightarrow$ $x^3-8x^2-2x^2+16x=0$
