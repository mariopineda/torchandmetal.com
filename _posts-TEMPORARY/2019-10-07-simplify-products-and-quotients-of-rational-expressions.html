Simplify the following rational expressions.

  1. $$\displaystyle\frac{x^2-9}{6x+24} \times \displaystyle\frac{10x+40}{x(x+3)}$$
  2. $$\displaystyle\frac{x-5}{2x} \times \displaystyle\frac{4x^2}{2x-10}$$
  3. $$\displaystyle\frac{m-8}{m^2} \times 2m^2$$
  4. $$\displaystyle\frac{6x^2}{15} \div \displaystyle\frac{12x}{30x^3}$$
  5. $$\displaystyle\frac{3b+9}{b+3} \div \displaystyle\frac{b}{b}$$
  6. $$\displaystyle\frac{2y^3-4y^2}{3y^2-9y} \div \displaystyle\frac{y^2-4}{(y+2)(y-3)}$$
{% capture solution %}

A.

$$ \begin{array}{l} \displaystyle\frac{x^2-9}{6x+24} \times \displaystyle\frac{10x+40}{x(x+3)} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{(x+3)(x-3)}{6(x+4)} \times \displaystyle\frac{10(x+4)}{x(x+3)} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{x-3}{6} \times \displaystyle\frac{10}{x} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{x-3}{3} \times \displaystyle\frac{5}{x} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{5(x-3)}{3x}, x \neq -4, -3, 0 \end{array} $$

B.

$$ \begin{array}{l} \displaystyle\frac{x-5}{2x} \times \displaystyle\frac{4x^2}{2x-10} \\ =\displaystyle\frac{x-5}{2x} \times \displaystyle\frac{2x}{2} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{2x}{2} \\ = x, x \neq 0,5 \end{array} $$

C.

$$ \begin{array}{l} \displaystyle\frac{m-8}{m^2} \times 2m^2 \\ = 2(m-8), m \neq 0 \end{array} $$

D.

$$ \begin{array}{l} \displaystyle\frac{6x^2}{15} \div \displaystyle\frac{12x}{30x^3} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{6x^2}{15} \times \displaystyle\frac{30x^3}{12x} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{x}{1} \times \displaystyle\frac{2x^3}{2} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{2x^4}{2} \\ = x^4, x \neq 0 \end{array} $$

E.

$$ \begin{array}{l} \displaystyle\frac{3b+9}{b+3} \div \displaystyle\frac{b}{b} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{3b+9}{b+3} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{3(b+3)}{b+3} \\ = 3, b \neq -3, 0 \end{array} $$

F.

$$ \begin{array}{l} \displaystyle\frac{2y^3-4y^2}{3y^2-9y} \div \displaystyle\frac{y^2-4}{(y+2)(y-3)} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{2y^3-4y^2}{3y^2-9y} \times \displaystyle\frac{(y+2)(y-3)}{y^2-4} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{2y^2(y-2)}{3y(y-3)} \times \displaystyle\frac{(y+2)(y-3)}{(y+2)(y-2)} \\ = \displaystyle\frac{2y}{3}, y \neq -2,0,2,3 \end{array} $$
