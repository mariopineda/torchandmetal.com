The length of a rectangle is $2x+5$ and the width is $3x-2$

  1. Determine a simplified expression to represent the area of this rectangle.
  2. If $x=3$ cm, determine the area of this rectangle using two methods.
  3. If the rectangle is a square, determine the length of one side.
{% if false %}

A.

\[ \begin{align} & (9a+4)+(2a+2)+(a-4) \\ = & 9a+4+2a+2+a-4 \\ = & 12a + 2 \end{align} \]

B.

\[ \begin{align} 12a+2 & = 38 \\ 12a & = 36 \\ a & = 36 \div 12 \\ a & = 3 \end{align} \]
