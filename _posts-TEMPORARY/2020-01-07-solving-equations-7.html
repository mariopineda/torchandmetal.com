Solve:

  1. $$\fraction{50}{u}-(-2)=7$$
  2. $$\fraction{21}{z}+3=6$$
  3. $$\fraction{35}{y}-9=-14$$
  4. $$\fraction{-32}{z}+6=14$$
  5. $$3-\fraction{-32}{c}=-5$$

{% capture solution %}

A.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} \fraction{50}{u}-(-2) & = & 7 \\ \fraction{50}{u}+2 & = & 7 \\ \fraction{50}{u} & = & 7 -2 \\ \fraction{50}{u} & = & 5 \\ \fraction{u}{50} & = & \fraction{1}{5} \\ u & = & \fraction{1}{5} \times 50 \\ u & = & 10 \end{array} \]

B.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} \fraction{21}{z}+3 & = & 6 \\ \fraction{21}{z} & = & 6 - 3 \\ \fraction{21}{z} & = & 3 \\ \fraction{z}{21} & = & \fraction{1}{3} \\ x & = & \fraction{1}{3} \times 21 \\ x & = & 7 \end{array} \]

C.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} \fraction{35}{y}-9 & = & -14 \\ \fraction{35}{y} & = & -14+9 \\ \fraction{35}{y} & = & -5 \\ \fraction{y}{35} & = & -\fraction{1}{5} \\ y & = & -\fraction{1}{5} \times 35 \\ y & = & -7 \end{array} \]

D.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} \fraction{-32}{z}+6 & = & 14 \\ \fraction{-32}{z} & = & 14-6 \\ \fraction{-32}{z} & = & 8 \\ \fraction{-32}{z} & = & 8 \\ \fraction{z}{-32} & = & \fraction{1}{8} \\ z & = & \fraction{1}{8} (-32) \\ z & = & -4 \end{array} \]

E.

\[ \begin{array}{rcl} 3-\fraction{-32}{c} & = & -5 \\ -\fraction{-32}{c} & = & -5-3 \\ \fraction{-32}{c} & = & -8 \\ \fraction{c}{-32} & = & -\fraction{1}{8} \\ c & = & -\fraction{1}{8} \times (-32) \\ c & = & 4 \end{array} \] {% endcapture %} {% include_relative solution.html content=solution %}
