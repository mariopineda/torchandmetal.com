Solve each equation. Check for extraneous roots.

  1. $\displaystyle\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{3x-2}=4$
  2. $\sqrt{10x+6}=-4$
  3. $\sqrt{33-6x}+4=13$
{% if true %}

A.

\[ \begin{align} \displaystyle\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{3x-2} & = 4 \\ \sqrt{3x-2} & = 8 \\ 3x-2 & = 64 \\ 3x & = 66 \\ x & = 22 \end{align} \] Verify: $\displaystyle\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{3 \times 22-2} = 4$, $LHS = RHS \Rightarrow$ Valid root

B.

\[ \begin{align} \sqrt{10x+6} & = -4 \\ 10x+6 & = 8 \\ 10x & = 10 \\ x & = 1 \end{align} \] Verify: $\sqrt{10 \times 1+6} \neq -4$, $LHS \neq RHS \Rightarrow$ Extraneous root

C.

\[ \begin{align} \sqrt{33-6x}+4 & = 13 \\ \sqrt{33-6x} & = 9 \\ 33-6x & = 81 \\ -6x & = 48 \\ x & = -8 \end{align} \] Verify: $\sqrt{33-6(-8)}+4 = 13 $, $LHS = RHS \Rightarrow$ Valid root
