  1. $(7a^3b)(4a^2b^5)$
  2. $(-12mn^3)(3mn^2)$
  3. $(-9x^2y^3)(-5x^2)$
  4. $(2x^3y^2)^3(-8x^4y)$
  5. $(5mn^2)^2(-3m^3n)^2$
  6. $(5mn^2)(-3m^2n)^2$
  7. $(-2x^4y^3)^5(-4xy^2)^2$
  8. $\left(-\displaystyle\frac{1}{3}ab^2c^3 \right)^2 \times \left(-\displaystyle\frac{1}{6}cb^2a^3 \right)^3$
  9. $((xy)^2)^3 (3yx)$
  10. $(3p^3q)(-2p^2)\left(-\displaystyle\frac{1}{6}q^3\right)$
