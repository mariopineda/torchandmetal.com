Expand and simplify the following polynomials.

  1. $3[5a-(2-4b)]+6x[2a-(5)+3b]$
  2. $5a[2a+3(4a-9)]+3a[a-7(2a+6)]$
  3. $2x[5x-(4+7y)]-6x[3x+(2y-5)]$
  4. $8x[2x-3y(x+2)]-5x[4x-7y(2x-9)]$

A.

\[ \begin{align} & (9a+4)+(2a+2)+(a-4) \\ = & 9a+4+2a+2+a-4 \\ = & 12a + 2 \end{align} \]

B.

\[ \begin{align} 12a+2 & = 38 \\ 12a & = 36 \\ a & = 36 \div 12 \\ a & = 3 \end{align} \]
