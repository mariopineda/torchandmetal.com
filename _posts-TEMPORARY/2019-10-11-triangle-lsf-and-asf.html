$\triangle DEF$ is an enlargement of $\triangle ABC$ where $AB=14$, $AB + DE = 35$ cm and the area of $\triangle DEF = 144$ cm$^2$.

  1. Determine the linear scale factor that relates $\triangle DEF$ to $\triangle ABC$.
  2. Determine the area of $\triangle ABC$.
{% if true %}
  1. $DE=35-14=21$ cm, $LSF=\displaystyle\frac{21}{14}=\displaystyle\frac{3}{2}$
  2. $ASF=\left(\displaystyle\frac{3}{2}\right)^2 = \displaystyle\frac{9}{4} \Rightarrow$ $\displaystyle\frac{9}{4} = \displaystyle\frac{144}{x} \Rightarrow$ $x=\displaystyle\frac{144 \times 4}{9} = 64$
{% endif %}
