Write an expression that represents the following:

  • A. an even number
  • B. an odd number
  • C. two odd numbers
  • D. three odd numbers
  • E. two consecutive odd numbers
  • F. three consecutive odd numbers
  • G. two even numbers
  • H. two consecutive even numbers
  • I. three even numbers
  • J. three consecutive even numbers
  • K. three consecutive integers
  • L. the square of an odd number
  • M. the sum of the squares of two odd numbers

{% capture solution %}

Note: There are several possible answers for each question.

  • A. $2n$
  • B. $2n+1$
  • C. $2n+1$ and $2n+3$
  • D. $2n+-1$, $2n+1$, $2n+3$
  • E. $2n+1$ and $2n+3$
  • F. $2n+-1$, $2n+1$, $2n+3$
  • G. $2n$ and $2n+2$
  • H. $2n$ and $2n+2$
  • I. $2n$, $2n-2$ and $2n+2$
  • J. $2n$, $2n-2$ and $2n+2$
  • K. $n$, $n+1$ and $n+2$
  • L. $(2n+1)^2$
  • M. $(2n+1)^2+(2n+3)^2$
