Classroom Expectations
  • Everyone has chosen to come here to learn.
  • Your teacher is here to help you learn.
  • Music and cell phones are distracting during lessons - both for you and others. Earbuds are not allowed during instructional time and during exams.
  • Watching videos or playing video games is not permitted during class.
  • Be punctual and attend regularly.
  • If you are late, enter quietly without disturbing the class and sign in.
  • If you need to leave class early, please let your teacher know beforehand.
  • Keep complete and neat notes.
  • Ask questions, seek assistance and be an active participant in class.
  • Complete assignments in a timely manner.
