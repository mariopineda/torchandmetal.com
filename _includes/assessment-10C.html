Exam Weights
The exams are weighted as follows:
  • Unit Finals: 80%
  • Final Exam: 20%
Replacement Exams
Replacement Exams provide an opportunity for students to re-challenge previously evaluated outcomes. Replacement exams are offered during the course on the dates indicated in the timeline.
Missed Exams
You are expected to write tests on the scheduled dates. Failure to meet this expectation will result in a referral to the student support team or to the administration. There are no late writes on replacement exams.
